Wallet

What are Wallets?

São repositorios de cartões e dados de pagamentos para consumidores online. As Carteiras digitais permitem que um consumidor realizar o cadastro de seus dados de pagamento, asYes agilizando o processo de compra em lojas habilitadas em suas compras por possuir apenas um cadastro.

Wallets are credit card repositories for consumers. Wallets allow a consumer to register their payment data, thus streamlining the purchase process.

  • To use Online Wallets on the Cielo E-commerce API, the merchant must be integrated or be register in the desired Wallet first*.

We suggest contacting the Wallet’s technical support for more information on how to register on their system.

Available Wallets

The Cielo Ecommerce API has the following wallets integrated:

Wallet
Apple Pay
Samsung Pay
Android Pay
VisaCheckout
MasterPass
Atenção: When the "Wallet" node is sent the "CreditCard" node becomes optional.
Atenção: For the debit card payments using "Wallets", the "DebitCard" node containing the "ReturnUrl" is required
Atenção:Due to the use, by the Wallets, of "ephemeral keys" to carry credit card information, Recurrence is not available for Wallets transactions

Basic Integration

As wallets na Api Cielo E-commerce possuem duas maneiras de utilização. The Cielo Ecommerce API has two kinds of integration

  1. Decryption - When the merchant repasses the information received from the Wallet to Cielo. The API is responsible for decrypting the payment information.
  2. PAN/Card Sending - When the merchant decrypts the information received from the Wallet and sends the PAN/Card number and CAVV/ECI codes to Cielo

Apple Pay

Prerequisites

To use Apple Pay on the Cielo E-commerce API it’s required that the merchant have a MerchantIdentifier with Apple The ** Decryption ** integration requires the merchant to manually upload a CSR certificate (in PEM format) provided by Cielo. Contact the Cielo product team to obtain the Certificate.

MerchantIdentifier

To obtain the MerchantIdentifier follow the steps:

  1. Log in into Apple Developer
  2. Select Certificates, IDs & Profiles
  3. In the “Identifiers” area, click on “Merchant IDs”
  4. Click on +, below “Registering a Merchant ID”
  5. Define a description to the MerchantID and the identifier. Example: “merchant.com.CIELO.merchantAccount”
  6. Click on “next” and check if the information shown are correct.

The MerchantIdentifier must be sent to Cielo, so a CSR certificate can be created.

CSR Certificate

After send the MerchantIdentifier to Cielo, the merchant will receive a CSR Certificate. This certificate must be uploaded to Apples System:

  1. Log in at Apple Developer
  2. Select Certificates, IDs & Profiles
  3. manually upload the certificate.
